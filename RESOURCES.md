## What TUUCAN is

**Tennessee Unitarian Universalist Collective Action Network** — a statewide UU justice
ministry and legislative advocacy network whose membership reflects UU communities
across Tennessee. (Jesse confirmed 2026-06-27 the "C" stands for **Collective** —
i.e. Tennessee + UU + Collective + Action + Network. Earlier drafts said "Community";
that was wrong. Some external sources also render it "Tennessee UU Congregational
Action Network" — disregard.)

Self-description used in announcements: "a statewide collective of Unitarian
Universalists living and amplifying our shared values of justice, equity,
transformation, plurality, interdependence, and generosity" — with love at the center.

**Purpose:** Tennessee UUs working together collectively — pooling resources and
helping each other to face issues that affect the whole state, such as natural
disasters and legislative concerns. Activities include legislative advocacy,
community mobilization, resource sharing among congregations, and statewide
community building.

## History & structure

- Formed after ~2 years of development; founding/formative retreat in **January 2025**
  with representation from nearly all **14 Tennessee UU congregations**.
- Governed by a **steering committee** (formative as of 2025; governance and vision
  were on the agenda at the Fall 2025 gathering).
- **Meetings:** 3rd Saturday of each month, 10 AM, via Zoom.
- Member of **CUUSAN** (see below).

## Signature event: Fall Flock

Annual statewide in-person gathering ("Flock" — fits the toucan branding).
2025 edition: **October 17–19, 2025** at First UU Church of Nashville (FUUN),
1808 Woodmont Blvd, Nashville; Friday dinner at Greater Nashville UU Congregation
(GNUUC), 374 Hicks Rd, Nashville; Sunday service at FUUN. Hybrid (in-person + Zoom),
travel assistance and home hospitality offered. Program: breakout sessions, clergy
panel on state issues, CUUSAN representation, sacred circles, action planning.
Primary 2025 focus area: **climate justice**.

## Parent/umbrella: CUUSAN

**Coalition of Unitarian Universalist State Action Networks** — https://cuusan.org
(site blocks automated fetching; browse manually).

- Formed 2015–2016. An informal association of UU state witness/advocacy/action
  organizations "from Florida to Washington and Maine to California."
- Purpose: support the establishment, growth, and development of UU State Action
  Networks (SANs); advocate for SANs with national UU partner organizations.
- Offers resources on organizing, engaging UUs, fundraising/grants, communication,
  social media, technology, and advocacy — useful reference material for TUUCAN.
- The UUA coordinates annual SAN professional development with CUUSAN the day before
  General Assembly. The UUA/UUSC "Love Resists" campaign and **Side With Love** work
  closely with SANs.
